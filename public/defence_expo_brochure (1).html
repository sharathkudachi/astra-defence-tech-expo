01 About the Institution BMSIT&M
About BMSIT&M
BMS Institute of Technology & Management
Yelahanka, Bengaluru – 560064  ·  Est. 2003
VTU Affiliated · AICTE Approved
BMS Institute of Technology and Management (BMSIT&M), Bengaluru, is a premier engineering institution established under the BMS Educational Trust. Affiliated with Visvesvaraya Technological University (VTU) and approved by AICTE, the institute is recognized for its strong academic foundation, industry-oriented learning, and commitment to fostering innovation and research.
With modern infrastructure, advanced laboratories, and experienced faculty, BMSIT&M offers a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs in engineering and technology. The institution continuously promotes practical learning, interdisciplinary collaboration, and real-world problem solving — making it a hub for emerging engineers and innovators.

03 About the Event BMSIT&M
About the Expo
The Defence Technology Expo 2026 is a dynamic, one-day inter-college platform designed to bring together student innovators, industry professionals, startups, and defence experts under one roof. The event is structured to create a real-world ecosystem where academic innovation meets industry expectations.
Student teams from 20+ colleges across Bengaluru will present working models, prototypes, and conceptual solutions. In addition, companies and startups working in defence and technology sectors will showcase their products and technologies, enabling direct interaction between students and industry.

05 Day Schedule BMSIT&M
Event Workflow
A carefully structured schedule to ensure smooth execution, maximum engagement, and zero disruption between parallel activities.
9:00
AM · Start
📋 Registration & Stall Setup
All participants report to campus. Mandatory check-in before 9:30 AM. Stall allotment for student teams and companies. Volunteers assist in setup and directional guidance. Registration desks open.
10:30
AM
🎤 Inauguration Ceremony
Formal opening of the event. Welcome address by Principal / HOD. Introduction of distinguished guests and event overview. Kept brief and time-bound.
11:00
AM · Parallel
🛡️ Lecture Session 1 & Expo Launch (Parallel)
Seminar Hall: Brigadier BM Cariappa delivers his session (~1.5 hrs, uninterrupted, no entry/exit during session).  |  Expo Field: Student project stalls open. Companies begin showcasing. Visitors explore freely.
12:30
PM
🔍 Expert Evaluation Round 1
Judges from DRDO / ISRO visit stalls. Interact with student teams. Initial evaluation and shortlisting of top projects begins. Expo continues simultaneously.
1:00
PM · Break
🍽️ Lunch Break & Networking
Refreshment break for all participants. Informal networking between students, companies, and judges. High-value interaction window — encouraged for all participants.
2:00
PM · Parallel
💡 Keynote Session 2 & Final Expo (Parallel)
Seminar Hall: Afternoon keynote by invited industry/space expert (in discussion).  |  Expo Field: Final round of demonstrations. Companies and students in active interaction.
3:15
PM
⚖️ Final Evaluation & Result Preparation
Judges complete final scoring. Shortlisted projects reviewed in depth. Results prepared. Top innovations identified for recognition and cash prizes.
3:40
PM · Close
🏆 Closing Ceremony & Recognition
Announcement of top projects and cash prize distribution. Guest and judge remarks. Vote of thanks by organizing team. Official closure of Defence Technology Expo 2026.
